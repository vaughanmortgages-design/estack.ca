Best Prepaid & Gift Cards for Canadian Small Business

estack.caJune 20265 min read

Prepaid and gift cards aren't just for retail customers. Smart Canadian business owners use them to manage contractor payments, reward loyal clients, run promotions, and keep business spending separate from personal accounts.

Why Small Business Owners Use Prepaid Cards

  • Expense control — Give contractors a card with a set limit instead of reimbursements
  • Client gifts — A gift card is a clean, professional way to thank a referral source
  • Promotions — Offer a gift card incentive instead of a discount to protect your margin
  • Subscription tracking — Keep business subscriptions on a dedicated prepaid card

Using Gift Cards for Client Referrals

One of the highest-ROI moves for any self-employed Canadian is a structured referral program. When a client sends you a new customer, send them a $50 gift card. It costs less than one hour of your rate and keeps the referrals coming consistently.

CRA Tax Note

Client gift cards may be deductible as a business expense up to $25 per person per year under CRA guidelines. Consult your accountant — but don't skip the strategy because of the cap.
