Editorial Policy

Editorial standards

eStack.ca is built to help readers understand categories before clicking offers. We avoid exaggerated claims and keep partner content clearly identified.

Our principles

  • Use plain language.
  • Separate Canadian and U.S. content.
  • Present partner links as resources, not guarantees.
  • Include disclosures where affiliate compensation may apply.
  • Keep eStack as an independent publication with no personal or mortgage-brand references.

Affiliate content

Some links may be sponsored or affiliate links. Compensation does not change the goal of the page: helping readers compare options and understand the category.

Not financial advice

eStack.ca provides general information only. Readers should review terms directly with each provider and make decisions based on their own situation.
