About eStack.ca

eStack.ca is an independent Canadian publication for money, markets, tools, deals and practical financial resources.

What we cover

We organize useful information across Canadian finance, live markets, credit, loans, business software, AI tools, ecommerce, precious metals, gift cards and partner resources.

How we work

Our goal is to make comparisons easier. We explain categories in plain language, highlight risks where relevant, and present partner links as resources rather than hype.

Independent publication

eStack.ca is built as a standalone media brand. It is not a personal brand, mortgage brand, brokerage brand, or financial advisory service.
