💰 Tax & Reporting for Canadian Precious Metals Investors

Capital Gains: Precious metals are capital property. Gains above $250k/year taxed at 66.67% inclusion; first $250k at 50%.

GST/HST: Investment-grade precious metals (99.5%+ purity) are zero-rated — no sales tax on bullion coins and bars.

Record Keeping: The CRA requires documentation of all transactions. Save purchase receipts, certificates of authenticity, and storage records.
